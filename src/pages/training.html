Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our training programs

What prerequisites are required for your courses?

Prerequisites vary by course. For beginner courses, basic IT knowledge is typically sufficient. For advanced courses, we recommend prior experience with the relevant cloud platform or technology. Each course page lists specific prerequisites.

Do you provide hands-on labs and practice environments?

Yes, all our courses include hands-on labs and access to cloud environments for practical experience. We believe in learning by doing, so practical exercises are a core component of our training methodology.

Are certification exams included in the course fee?

Certification exam fees are not included in the standard course price. However, we offer exam vouchers at discounted rates for our students. Some of our premium packages do include exam vouchers.

What is your pass guarantee policy?

We offer a certification pass guarantee for most of our courses. If you don't pass the exam on your first attempt after completing our course, you can retake the training at no additional cost. Terms and conditions apply.

How are the online courses delivered?

Our online courses are delivered through a combination of live instructor-led sessions, pre-recorded videos, hands-on labs, and interactive assignments. We use a state-of-the-art learning management system that allows you to track your progress and interact with instructors and peers.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied with the course?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee for most of our courses. If you're not satisfied with the quality of training within the first week, you can request a full refund. Please refer to our refund policy for specific terms and conditions.

Have Questions About Our Training Programs?

Contact our training advisors to discuss your learning goals and find the right course for you.
